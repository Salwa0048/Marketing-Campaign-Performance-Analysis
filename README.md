# 📊 Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Description

This project focuses on analyzing 200,000 marketing campaign records to evaluate campaign effectiveness and identify business opportunities for improving marketing performance.

The analysis was conducted using Python (Pandas) for data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), KPI generation, and business insights extraction. The processed dataset was then visualized in Power BI through an interactive dashboard.

The primary goal of this project was to answer key business questions such as:

* Which marketing channels generate the highest ROI?
* Which campaign types perform best?
* Which customer segments are most profitable?
* Which locations produce the strongest marketing results?
* Do clicks and impressions actually lead to conversions?

By transforming raw campaign data into actionable insights, this project demonstrates a complete end-to-end data analytics workflow from data exploration to dashboard development.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Marketing teams invest heavily in multiple advertising channels and campaign types. However, identifying which strategies generate the best returns can be challenging.

This project aims to:

* Measure campaign effectiveness.
* Compare marketing channels.
* Evaluate customer segment performance.
* Identify high-performing locations.
* Generate data-driven recommendations for future marketing investments.

## 🔍 Key Findings

### Channel Performance

* Facebook achieved the highest average ROI.
* Email campaigns generated the highest conversion rate.
* ROI remained relatively consistent across channels.

### Customer Segments

* Foodies delivered the strongest ROI.
* Tech Enthusiasts closely followed.

### Campaign Types

* Influencer campaigns produced the highest ROI.
* Influencer campaigns also achieved the highest conversion rate.

### Geographic Performance

* Miami generated the highest ROI.
* Los Angeles and Houston performed strongly.

### User Behavior Insights

* Clicks showed almost no correlation with conversion rate.
* Higher impressions do not necessarily result in higher conversions.
* Marketing success depends on campaign quality rather than traffic volume alone.

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Recommendation 1

Increase investment in Facebook campaigns due to their superior ROI performance.

### Recommendation 2

Expand Influencer marketing strategies because they consistently achieve strong ROI and conversion rates.

### Recommendation 3

Prioritize high-performing customer segments such as Foodies and Tech Enthusiasts.

### Recommendation 4

Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions including Miami and Los Angeles.

### Recommendation 5

Improve conversion optimization strategies instead of solely increasing impressions and clicks.
